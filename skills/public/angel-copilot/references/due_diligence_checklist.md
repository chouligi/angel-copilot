# AngelCopilot Due Diligence Checklist

Use this as a structured guide before investing in any startup.

## 1. Team and Founders
- Full-time, complementary founders
- Prior startup or industry experience
- Clear roles and equity split
- Reputation check (LinkedIn, media, references)

## 2. Problem and Solution
- Clear pain point and paying users
- Early validation (pilot, waitlist, LOI)
- Differentiation vs competition

## 3. Product and Tech
- MVP or product live
- Technical ownership (in-house dev team)
- Tech defensibility (IP, data, automation)

## 4. Market
- Large, growing market (TAM/SAM/SOM clarity)
- Strong timing ("why now")
- Competitive awareness

## 5. Traction and Metrics
- MRR or key user growth
- Retention or churn data
- CAC, LTV, payback period
- Notable customers or logos

## 6. Financials and Terms
- Reasonable burn and runway
- Cap table cleanliness
- Valuation aligned with stage
- Rights: pro-rata, info rights

## 7. Legal and Risks
- Registered entity and IP ownership
- No major pending legal disputes
- Clear fundraising documents (SAFE, SHA)

Use this checklist before or alongside AngelCopilot's automatic assessment.
